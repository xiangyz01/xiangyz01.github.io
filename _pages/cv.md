Research Experience
======
* **Safety-Critical Nonlinear Control for Complex Systems (RL, Fault-Tolerant, Reliability-Aware)**  
  *Leader, Postgraduate Research & Practice Innovation Program of Jiangsu Province*
  * Developed reinforcement learning-based nonlinear controllers with prescribed-performance envelopes to shape transient behavior and guarantee fixed-time convergence under uncertainties.
  * Designed event-triggered integral reinforcement learning schemes to reduce update and communication burden while maintaining stability and performance guarantees.
  * Conducted PMSM design and optimization via multi-physics finite element analysis, and validated transient safety/reliability performance through ANSYS Twin Builder co-simulation and dSPACE HIL experiments.

* **Safety-Critical Cooperative Control for Multi-Agent Nonlinear Systems (Switched, Distributed)**  
  *Participant, National Natural Science Foundation of China*
  * Developed distributed reinforcement learning-based optimal control methods for switched nonlinear multi-agent systems, with emphasis on safe switching, stability, and reliability under mode-dependent uncertainties.
  * Developed safe formation control schemes for multi-agent systems integrating optimality and safety constraints, supported by simulation and performance-guarantee analysis.
